Let $f: \mathbb{R}^d\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ be a convex function, i.e.,\[f(\theta x + (1-\theta) y) \leq \theta f(x) + (1-\theta) f(y),\]for all $x,y\in\mathbb{R}^d$ and $\theta\in[0,1]$.
The convex conjugate $f^\ast:\mathbb{R}^d\rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ is defined as follows: \[ f^\ast(\alpha)=\sup_{x\in\mathbb{R}^d}\left(\langle \alpha, x\rangle - f(x)\right). \]
